Products

We deal 8 Products

  1. Cup cone set/ball racer set: It is a set of bearings that helps in steering and balancing the front wheel of a two-wheeler. The set includes a cup, a cone and ball bearings

  2. Side stand: A side stand is a metal support that is attached to the side of a two-wheeler and is used to hold the vehicle upright when it is parked. It is foldable with spring-loaded, and can be easily retracted when not in use.

  3. Center stand: A center stand is support used to hold a two-wheeler upright when parked. It can be used to lift the rear wheel off the ground for maintenance or repair work.

  4. Seat handle (Seat kunda): A seat handle, is a metal handle attached to the rear side of a two-wheeler’s seat. It is used to lift and hold the seat in an open position to access the storage space or the engine compartment.

  5. Footrest rod: A footrest rod is a metal rod that supports the footrests on a two-wheeler. It is usually made of steel or aluminum and is attached to the frame of the vehicle.

  6. Footrest assembly: A footrest assembly is a complete unit that includes the footrest rod, footrest bracket, rubber footrest pads. It provides a comfortable place for the rider to rest their feet while riding,
